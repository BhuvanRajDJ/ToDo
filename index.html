Welcome to Aurora

Welcome to Aurora, your personal companion for productivity and atomic habit building. Your privacy and security are our absolute priority.

Local Storage Only

All your tasks, routines, habits, and journal entries are saved locally on your device's secure browser database (IndexedDB).

Zero Server Transmissions

Absolutely no data is ever uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any remote servers. Your thoughts and records are for your eyes only.

No Tracking or Sharing

This application does not collect, track, analyze, or share any personal details, metrics, or usage behavior. It is completely tracking-free.

Completely Offline

Aurora functions entirely offline, without requiring an active internet connection. Focus without distractions, completely private.
